Hackly is a place where developers of all levels come together to learn, build, and grow. Whether you’re self-taught, a student, or already working in tech, collaboration is one of the best ways to level up and create cool stuff.

Here, it’s all about teamwork and solving real problems. Over 1-2 weeks, you’ll work with others to build quick prototypes—and learn along the way. Focusing on versionability to hopefully turn them into practical projects.

A lot of open-source projects fail because creators have to carry everything on their own, before the project is usable, let alone attract contributors. At Hackly, we work as a community from the start to make better decisions and build things that actually last.

Jump in, meet awesome people, and create cool or fun things to make the world more beautiful, more fun, or just better. No focus on business value whatsoever and failure is ok and expected.
