Too many subscriptions (2025 edition)

January 2, 2025

It’s a new year and I’m between jobs, which means it’s doubly important that we eliminate recurring bills we don’t need right now. We can always resub later.

Here’s what we’ve axed so far:

  • Consumer Reports (sad but we I don’t need to be looking at cars right now)
  • Xbox Game Pass (I own plenty of single player games that I never finished, and let’s be honest, they’re likely gonna stay unfinished)
  • PS+ (expires in April and see above)
  • Netflix (cost has become absolutely outrageous. Arcane and ITYSL are the only good shows and they’re not enough)
  • Target (this cost us more money than if we just paid for delivery. Watch out for minimum order amounts)

I also looked at removing Apple One and YNAB, but Apple One is engineered to make unbundling and plan downgrades more expensive than keeping it, so we’re keeping it. And there really aren’t any other budgeting apps that fit as well as YNAB. If I find a cross-platform budgeting app with bank imports, decent mobile apps, a solid future, and no slop engines (AI), I’ll let you know.
