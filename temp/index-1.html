About Event

​** While all seats are currently occupied, we understand that some individuals may be unable to attend. If you are interested in attending, please sign up for our waitlist, and we will contact you immediately if a seat becomes available.

Join us for an exclusive showcase of South Korea's most promising startups at the heart of Silicon Valley.

  • ​Date: Tuesday, October 29, 2024

  • ​Time: 2:30 – 6:00 PM (Networking and dinner to follow)

  • ​Location: O'Melveny & Myers LLP office, Menlo Park, CA (2765 Sand Hill Rd, Menlo Park, CA 94025)

​About the Event

​The Korea Venture Business Association (KOVA) and beSUCCESS Media Group present a delegation of top Korean startups as part of the 2024 Gangnam Global Roadshow. This Demo Day offers investors, VCs, and corporate leaders a unique opportunity to explore cutting-edge innovation from Korea's thriving startup ecosystem.

​Event Agenda

  • ​2:00 PM - 2:30 PM: Welcome Reception

  • ​2:30 PM - 3:00 PM: Welcome Speech and VC Introductions

  • ​3:00 PM – 4:30 PM: Startup Presentations (5-minute pitch + 5-minute feedback each)

  • ​4:30 PM – 6:00 PM: Networking Reception (Catering)

​Featured Startups

​[Technology & Software Solutions]

  1. ​Impact AI – AI-powered advertising optimization platform, OASIS.

  2. ​INOFriends – Fintech solutions enhancing asset management and security.

​[Healthcare, Bio, & Wellness]

  1. ​The StemBank – Innovators in stem cell-based beautytech.

  2. ​Bilix – Biotechnology advancements with Brixelle® for oxidative stress protection.

​[Digital Media & Entertainment]

  1. ​xCREW – A seamless platform for global travel experiences.

  2. ​Sweetndata – AI-driven human digital twin solutions for media and entertainment.

​[Smart Infrastructure & Mobility Solutions]

  1. ​Contactus – AI and AIoT-powered property management solutions.

  2. ​BANF – Revolutionizing logistics with real-time tire safety technology.

​Who Should Attend?

​This event is designed for investors, corporate representatives, procurement specialists, and anyone interested in exploring Korean startups and their innovative solutions.

​Why Attend?

  • ​Exclusive access to eight innovative Korean startups

  • ​Network with global VCs and industry leaders

  • ​Explore strategic investment opportunities in emerging technologies

​Don't miss this chance to witness the future of technology and business from Korea's dynamic startup scene.
